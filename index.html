About Cangem

Cangem USA is the United States subsidiary of Cangem Global, a multinational privately-held consulting corporation active in international/foreign trade, international logistics (bulk vessels & containers) and commodities trading consulting. Cangem has offices in key time zones around the globe and provides discrete consulting services to private and public sector entities, with a focus on USA markets.
